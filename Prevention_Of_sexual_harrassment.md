## What kinds of behaviour cause sexual harassment?
* Comments about someone’s body, appearance, or private life
* Repeated requests for dates after a refusal
* Sending inappropriate messages, emojis, or emails
* Blocking someone’s movement or standing too close
* Any form of forced physical contact

## What would you do in case you face or witness any incident or repeated incidents of such behaviour?

* Say NO clearly and tell the person the behavior is unwelcome.
* Move away from the situation immediately.
* Record details like date, time, place, messages, and witnesses.
* Save evidence (screenshots, emails, chats, call logs).
* Inform a trusted person like friends, family
