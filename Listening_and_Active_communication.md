## What are the steps/strategies to do Active Listening? (Minimum 6 points)
    * Avoid distraction by your own thoughts.
    * Focus on the topic of the speaker.
    * Don't interrupt another person when they are speaking. Speak once they finish.
    * Give an interesting response when they are speaking to show your interest.
    * Maintain eye contact.
    * Body language.

## According to Fisher's model, what are the key points of Reflective Listening? 
    * Listen Actively
    * Stay calm, patient, and open-minded.
    * Encourage the Speaker to Continue by noding

## What are the obstacles in your listening process?
    * I often start thinking about something else while the other person is speaking.
    * This internal distraction affects my ability to fully focus and understand the message.
    * Noise or phone notifications can easily take my attention away from the conversation.

## What can you do to improve your listening?
    * Maintain eye contact and listen without interrupting.
    * Keep my phone aside or keep in silent mode.
    * Whenever I get distracted, bring my focus back to the speaker.
    * Taking notes when needed.

## When do you switch to Passive communication style in your day to day life?
    * When I don't want to heart someone's feeling
    * When someone is upset.
    * When I feel uncomfortable.

## When do you switch into Aggressive communication styles in your day to day life?
    * When someone lies.
    * When I feel stressed.
    * If someone is ignoring me, when I'm speaking something important to them.

## When do you switch into Passive Aggressive (sarcasm/gossiping/taunts/silent treatment and others) communication styles in your day to day life?
    * When someone irritates me.
    * The moment I feel ignored.
