## Question 1
What is Deep Work?

Deep work means working on something important with full focus without distractions.

## Question 2
According to author how to do deep work properly, in a few points?

* Eliminate distractions
* Create routines and rituals
* Train your focus muscle by not switching attention the moment you feel distracted. 
* Reduce social media/emails that pull your attention away.

## Question 3
How can you implement the principles in your day to day life?

* Removing distractions like silent phone and notification.
* During Deep Work, no social media or chat.Stay on one task only.
* After Deep Work, take a short break (walk, stretch).
* Reviewing weekly progress

## Question 4
What are the dangers of social media, in brief?

* Breaks our focus
* Creates addiction
* Increases stress & anxiety
* Reduces real-life interaction
* Lowers sleep quality
