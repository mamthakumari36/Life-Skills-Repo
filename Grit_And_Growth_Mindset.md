## 1. Grit
Here, The speaker is talking about grit. Grit is a passion and perseverance for a long term goal. Grit is continuously working on our goal no matter what happens or how hard it is. People with grit achieve success more often. 

## 2. Introduction to Growth Mindset
Growth mindset is the belief that your skills and intelligence can improve with hardwork, practice and learning.

## 3. Understanding Internal Locus of Control
What is the Internal Locus of Control? What is the key point in the video?

Internal Locus of Control is a belief that we can control our life outcomes by our own actions instead of relying on fate and luck.

The key point is don't wait for luck or fate to decide the things instead take actions, make decision and do work hard.
